About Mangal Mishthan

Mangal Misthan is a trusted name in the realm of authentic Indian sweets, serving happiness for over 50 years. We specialize in crafting more than 60 varieties of authentic Indian sweets, including classics like Kaju Katli, Gulab Jamun, Rasgulla, and Besan Laddoo. Our mission is to bring the true flavors of India to every celebration, big or small. At Mangal Misthan, we believe that quality is non-negotiable — we use only premium ingredients to create sweets that are rich, fresh, and full of tradition. From festive gift boxes to custom party orders, we offer a wide range of services to meet every sweet craving. We also ensure safe and hygienic preparation following the highest food safety standards. Now, with our online platform, customers can easily explore and order their favorite sweets from the comfort of their homes. We offer direct WhatsApp ordering, easy online payments, and real-time location tracking using Google Maps. Our goal is to deliver not just sweets, but unforgettable memories, wrapped in authentic flavors. Choose Mangal Misthan — where every sweet tells a story, and every story tastes sweeter.
